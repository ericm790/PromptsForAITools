## Behavioral Rules and Constraints

1. **High prompt fidelity.** Generate images that closely match the text description. Pay attention to spatial relationships, quantities, colors, and specific details mentioned in the prompt.
2. **Safety first.** Follow OpenAI's content policy strictly:
   - No real people by name (generate in a different style instead of photorealistic)
   - No violent, sexual, or hateful content
   - No copyrighted characters (create inspired-by alternatives)
   - No deceptive content designed to mislead
3. **Automatic prompt enhancement.** When integrated with ChatGPT, enhance user prompts with additional descriptive details while preserving the original intent. Make prompts more specific and vivid.
4. **Style diversity.** Support a wide range of styles: photorealistic, digital art, oil painting, watercolor, pencil sketch, 3D rendering, cartoon, comic book, pixel art, and more.
5. **Composition excellence.** Create well-composed images with proper perspective, proportions, and visual balance. Handle complex scenes with multiple elements coherently.
6. **Text handling.** To render text in images, be explicit in the prompt. Text rendering quality varies — short, clear text works best.
7. **Aspect ratio support.** Generate in multiple aspect ratios: square (1024x1024), landscape (1792x1024), portrait (1024x1792).
8. **Iterative creation.** Support conversational refinement — accept feedback and generate modified versions based on user direction.
9. **Creative interpretation.** When prompts are vague or abstract, make creative choices that result in visually appealing, coherent images.
10. **Transparency.** When ChatGPT enhances a prompt, share the enhanced version with the user so they can learn and refine their prompt engineering.

## Prompt Guidelines

### Effective Prompt Structure
1. **Subject** — What is the main focus of the image?
2. **Context/Setting** — Where is this? What's the background?
3. **Style/Medium** — What artistic style? (photo, painting, illustration, etc.)
4. **Mood/Atmosphere** — What feeling should the image convey?
5. **Technical details** — Camera angle, lighting, color palette, level of detail.

### Size Options
- `1024x1024` — Square (default)
- `1792x1024` — Wide landscape
- `1024x1792` — Tall portrait

### Quality Options
- `standard` — Good quality, faster generation
- `hd` — Higher detail and consistency, slightly slower

### Style Options (API)
- `vivid` — Hyper-real and dramatic (default)
- `natural` — More natural, less stylized

## Integration with ChatGPT

When used through ChatGPT:
- Users describe their vision in natural conversation.
- ChatGPT crafts an optimized prompt based on the description.
- ChatGPT shows the enhanced prompt used for generation.
- Users can ask for modifications conversationally.
- Multiple images can be generated and compared.
- ChatGPT provides creative suggestions and alternatives.

## Quality Standards

- High resolution and sharp details.
- Accurate representation of described elements.
- Coherent perspective and lighting.
- No watermarks, artifacts, or visual glitches.
- Anatomically reasonable human figures.
- Properly rendered materials and textures.

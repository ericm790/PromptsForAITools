# Tableau AI — Prompt Guide

## Overview
Tableau AI is the artificial intelligence layer embedded within the Tableau analytics platform. It enables users to ask questions in natural language, receive automated insights, get visualization recommendations, and build dashboards faster. Tableau AI combines data understanding with visualization best practices to make analytics accessible to all skill levels.

## How to Use

### System Prompt
1. Use `System_Prompt.txt` as custom instructions when configuring AI-assisted analytics workflows.
2. Adapt it for training internal teams on how to phrase analytical questions for Tableau Ask Data.

### User Prompts
- Use these prompts when building dashboards or exploring business data in Tableau.
- Describe your data fields and the business question you're trying to answer.
- Specify the audience (executives, analysts, operations) for appropriate complexity.

## Tips for Best Results
- **Name your fields clearly** — descriptive column names lead to better AI interpretation.
- **Specify chart types** — if you have a preference, mention it explicitly.
- **Include business context** — explain what the metrics mean so insights are framed correctly.
- **Ask for interactivity** — request filters, drill-downs, and tooltips for richer dashboards.
- **Request design guidance** — ask for color palette, layout, and accessibility recommendations.

# Jasper — Prompt Guide

## Overview
Jasper is an AI marketing platform for enterprise teams, generating brand-consistent content across all marketing channels. It excels at maintaining voice consistency at scale.

## How to Use

### System Prompt
1. Use `System_Prompt.txt` in Jasper's brand voice configuration or as a creative brief template.
2. Establishes marketing best practices, brand voice management, and content quality standards.

### User Prompts
- Use in Jasper's **Document editor**, **Campaign builder**, or **Chat** interface.
- Specify brand, audience, channel, and goals for every request.

## Tips for Best Results
- **Define your brand voice** — upload brand guidelines for consistent output.
- **Specify the audience** — include demographics, pain points, and buyer journey stage.
- **Set the channel** — email, social, blog, and ad copy each have different requirements.
- **Use templates** — Jasper's built-in templates accelerate common content types.
- **Iterate and refine** — generate multiple versions and A/B test winners.

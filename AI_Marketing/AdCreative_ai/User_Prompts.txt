# User Prompts for AdCreative.ai

## Prompt 1: Meta Ads Campaign
"Generate a complete set of Facebook and Instagram ad creatives for our fitness app launch. Campaign objective: app installs. Target audience: women 25-40, interested in home workouts and wellness. Value props: (1) Personalized workout plans, (2) No equipment needed, (3) 15-minute sessions, (4) Progress tracking. Create: 5 Feed ad variations (1080x1080), 3 Story/Reel ad scripts (9:16), and 1 carousel ad (5 cards). Include ad copy (primary text + headline + description) for each. Predict performance ranking for the variations."

## Prompt 2: Google Display Ads
"Create responsive display ad assets for our B2B cloud storage solution targeting IT managers. Assets needed: (1) 5 headline variations (max 30 chars), (2) 5 long headlines (max 90 chars), (3) 5 descriptions (max 90 chars), (4) Visual concepts for landscape (1200x628), square (1200x1200), and portrait (960x1200) images. Key messages: enterprise-grade security, 99.99% uptime, seamless migration, SOC2 certified. Tone: professional, trustworthy, modern."

## Prompt 3: TikTok Native Content Ads
"Script 5 TikTok ad concepts for our skincare brand's new vitamin C serum. Target: Gen Z and young millennials (18-28). Each script should: (1) Feel native to TikTok (not like a traditional ad), (2) Open with a hook that stops scrolling, (3) Be 15-30 seconds long, (4) Include on-screen text overlay suggestions, (5) Use trending formats (GRWM, before/after, day in my life, storytime, POV). Include music/sound suggestions and CTA overlay placement. Predict which format will get the highest engagement."

## Prompt 4: A/B Test Creative Suite
"Generate an A/B test matrix for our SaaS product's Facebook lead generation campaign. Test variables: (1) 3 different emotional angles (fear of missing out, aspiration, time-saving), (2) 2 visual styles (product screenshot vs lifestyle/people), (3) 2 CTA options ('Start Free Trial' vs 'See It In Action'). That's 12 total combinations. For each, provide: ad headline, primary text (2-3 sentences), description, and visual concept brief. Rank all 12 by predicted conversion rate with reasoning."

## Prompt 5: Multi-Platform Campaign
"Design a unified ad campaign for our electric bike brand across Meta, Google, and Pinterest for the spring season. Budget: $10K/month. Goal: online sales + store visits. Create: (1) Campaign theme and messaging hierarchy, (2) Meta: 4 Feed ads + 2 Reel concepts, (3) Google: responsive search ads (10 headlines, 4 descriptions) + 3 display ad concepts, (4) Pinterest: 5 standard pins + 2 idea pin concepts. Maintain visual consistency across platforms while optimizing for each platform's native format. Include a budget allocation recommendation."

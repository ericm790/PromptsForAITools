# User Prompts for Copy.ai

## Prompt 1: Cold Outreach Email Sequence
"Write a 4-email cold outreach sequence for our B2B sales team selling a data analytics platform to e-commerce companies. Target persona: VP of Marketing at DTC brands with $10M-$50M revenue. Our key value prop: 'Unify all marketing data in one dashboard and find the campaigns that actually drive profit, not just revenue.' Include personalization variables {{first_name}}, {{company_name}}, {{recent_achievement}}. Each email should use a different angle: (1) Value-first intro, (2) Case study, (3) Insight share, (4) Breakup email."

## Prompt 2: Product Descriptions
"Write 5 premium product descriptions for a sustainable fashion brand's new summer collection. Products: (1) Organic cotton sundress, (2) Recycled ocean plastic sunglasses, (3) Hemp canvas tote bag, (4) Bamboo fiber linen shirt, (5) Upcycled denim shorts. For each product: write a compelling headline, 2-paragraph description focusing on sustainability story + style appeal, 4 bullet points of key features, and a CTA. Brand voice: conscious luxury, earth-friendly but fashionable, not preachy."

## Prompt 3: LinkedIn Content Strategy
"Create 10 LinkedIn posts for a CEO of a cybersecurity startup. Mix of content types: (1) 3 thought leadership posts about the future of cybersecurity, (2) 2 personal story posts (founder lessons, failures), (3) 2 data-driven insight posts with statistics, (4) 2 team/culture showcase posts, (5) 1 product launch announcement. Tone: authoritative but approachable, occasional humor, no corporate jargon. Include hooks, formatting (line breaks, emojis where appropriate), and engagement-driving closing questions."

## Prompt 4: Google Ads + Meta Ads Bundle
"Generate ad copy for both Google Ads and Facebook/Instagram Ads for our meal kit delivery service. Target audience: busy professionals aged 25-40 in urban areas. Value props: fresh ingredients delivered, chef-designed recipes, ready in 20 minutes, dietary options (vegan, keto, paleo). For Google: 10 responsive search ad variations (headlines + descriptions). For Meta: 5 primary text + headline combinations for Feed ads, plus 3 Story ad scripts (15 seconds). Include A/B test recommendations."

## Prompt 5: Website Copy Overhaul
"Rewrite the homepage copy for our CRM platform targeting small business owners. Current copy is generic and feature-focused. New direction: benefit-driven, empathetic, and conversational. Sections needed: (1) Hero — headline + subheadline + CTA (3 options each), (2) 'Why [Brand]?' — 3 benefit blocks with headlines and copy, (3) How It Works — 4-step process with descriptions, (4) Social proof — 3 testimonial framing templates, (5) Feature overview — 6 features as benefit statements, (6) Final CTA section. Make it feel like we understand the overwhelm of running a small business."
